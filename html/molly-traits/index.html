Molly’s Traits

A scientific breakdown of vibes, fears, and heroic couch defense.

😨

Skittish

Molly reacts to unexpected sounds as if the universe has personally betrayed her. Crinkling bags, distant footsteps, and the suspicious existence of wind all trigger a full alert response.

😴

Sleepy

Naps are her primary occupation. Every surface is evaluated for softness, temperature, and emotional safety before being selected as today’s resting station.

🚁

Helicopter Tail

Her tail spins when excited, confused, or simply existing. It is both a mood indicator and a potential household hazard.

🧠

Deep Thinker

Molly stares into space contemplating things humans cannot perceive, possibly snacks in alternate dimensions.

👀

Hyper Aware

She notices everything, especially when she is pretending not to.

❤️

Loyal

Molly follows her people room to room, guarding them from invisible dangers and loneliness alike.
